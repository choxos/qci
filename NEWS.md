# qci 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* Complete pipeline from raw GBD CSV exports to normalized QCI scores (`qci_pipeline()`).
* Modular functions: `qci_load()`, `qci_clean()`, `qci_ratios()`, `qci_pca()`, `qci_gdr()`.
* Five visualization functions: `plot_qci_map()`, `plot_gdr_map()`, `plot_qci_trend()`, `plot_qci_distribution()`, `plot_qci_scatter()`.
* Gender Disparity Ratio (GDR) analysis.
* Export to CSV and Stata .dta formats.
* Bundled sample GBD data and location type metadata.
* Getting started vignette.
